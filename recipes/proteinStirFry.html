Protein Stir Fry

Home Page

Description:

Easy to make and tasty Protein Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs Or choice of Meat
  • Soy Sauce
  • Terikiyi Sause
  • Onion Powder
  • Rice
  • Mixed Vegetables
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Cooked mixed Vegetables and Choice of Meat
  2. Add rice to Mixed Vegetables and meat.
  3. Add a tablespoon of onion powder, a little soy sauce and Terikiyi sauce based on your liking.
  4. Mix it all up and let it cook for 5-10 mins.
  5. Then Add the Egg and cook for 1 min or to desired mixture.
  6. Then Enjoy the quick meal!
